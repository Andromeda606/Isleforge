Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Isleforge: Island Survival ("the app", "we") handles your information.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It runs fully offline. There is no account, no login, and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

Game progress (your save, settings, and crafted items) is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Third Parties

The app does not share data with any third party, and does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If a future version introduces features that collect data (for example, advertising), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be noted here.
